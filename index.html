Introducing Late fees in Sweden

The new designs play a pivotal role in the introduction of late fees in the Swedish market. When a customer's payment fails, an email is automatically sent to remind them to take action and make a payment to avoid late fees.

In this project, I led the way from design to implementation, working closely with the product team and conducting comprehensive email testing before the public launch. These efforts contributed significantly to Klarna's "get to net" initiative.
